Privacy Policy for Lili

Last Updated: May 6, 2026

At Lili, we are dedicated to helping you reach your fitness goals while keeping your personal data secure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a customized fitness experience, Lili collects:

  • Account Info: Name, email, and profile preferences.
  • Health Metrics: Weight, heart rate, activity logs, and calories burned.
  • Device Data: Integration with Apple Health, Google Fit, or wearable sensors.
  • Location: GPS data for tracking outdoor workouts (only with your permission).

2. How Your Data is Used

We use your information to:

  • Personalize your workout routines and fitness insights.
  • Track progress and celebrate your milestones.
  • Improve app performance and fix technical bugs.
  • Ensure the security of your account.

3. Data Sharing & Privacy

Your health is private. Lili does not sell your personal health data to third-party advertisers. We only share data with service providers necessary to run the app (like cloud storage) or when you explicitly choose to sync with external health platforms.

4. Your Rights

You have full control over your data within Lili. You may:

  • Access or export your fitness data at any time.
  • Correct or update your personal profile.
  • Delete your account: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data via the app settings.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. While we strive to use every commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
